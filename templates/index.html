About

LCKDWN Email Security Scanner is a defense and security tool designed to help IT professionals and security teams protect digital assets by analyzing domains, IPs, emails, and full email headers.

The scanner evaluates technical signals (SPF, DKIM, DMARC, DNS, WHOIS, IP geolocation) and provides a phishing risk score with AI reasoning for actionable insights.

All processing is private and compliant. Customer or sensitive data is never stored and external API calls are optional or fully offline.
